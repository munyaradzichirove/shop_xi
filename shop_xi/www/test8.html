What to know before choosing a package

We keep entry pricing simple, but every serious project still needs a quick scope discussion so the final delivery matches your business goals.

1

Starting price means basic scope

Extra pages, products, complex integrations, custom designs, advanced forms or dashboards may increase the final quote.

2

Hosting offers have limits

Free hosting applies to standard website packages. Heavy apps, ERP systems, e-commerce traffic or custom servers may need separate hosting.

3

Systems need proper scoping

ERP, accounting, web apps, automation and cybersecurity depend on workflows, users, data, integrations and support needs.
