Incident readiness

When something goes wrong, your team needs a plan.

We help businesses prepare practical response routines so incidents are handled faster, calmer and with less damage.

1

Detect

Know how to notice suspicious activity, login issues, unusual changes or service disruptions.

2

Contain

Limit damage by isolating affected accounts, systems, devices or access paths.

3

Recover

Restore services, validate backups, reset access and bring critical operations back safely.

4

Improve

Review what happened and strengthen controls to prevent the same issue from repeating.
